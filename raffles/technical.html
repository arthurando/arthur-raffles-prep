Technical Fluency

Entity & Relationship Modelling for Family Offices
▼

Family office CRM is a graph problem, not flat contact DB. Hierarchy:

  • Family Group → Members → Entities (trusts, SPVs, holdcos)
  • Entities → Accounts → Assets/Positions
  • Each node has compliance profile, jurisdiction, access controls

Polymorphic relationships: one person = beneficiary of Trust A, director of HoldCo B, LP in Fund C. This matters for:

  • Data visibility (RLS — who sees what)
  • Regulatory reporting (beneficial ownership, conflict tracking)
  • Performance attribution (entity-level vs. family-level)

Proof Point

STT Mall hierarchical customer segmentation — modelled 50K+ stores with ownership, franchise, and supply chain relationships.

Industry Reference

Salesforce FSC "household" objects, DealCloud/4Degrees graph-based platforms.

Compliance, Audit Trails & Data Governance
▼

Event-sourced audit trail: every state change = immutable event, not overwritten row. Non-negotiable in regulated environments (SFC, MAS).

Row-Level Security (RLS)

  • RMs see only their families
  • Compliance sees everything
  • Investment team sees deal pipeline
  • Leadership sees aggregated (no PII below C-level)

Regulatory Landscape

  • HK: SFC, PDPO, AMLO
  • Singapore: MAS, PDPA, CDSA
  • Cross-border: Data residency, conflict of law

Key Vocabulary

Suitability assessment, investment mandate tracking, beneficial ownership, KYC, AML, STR (suspicious transaction reporting).

Deal Flow as a State Machine
▼

Deterministic pipeline, not freeform. Stages:

  • Sourcing — Lead capture, source relationship
  • Initial Review — Mandate fit, quick pass/fail
  • DD — Deep dive, financials, legal, management
  • IC/Family Review — Investment Committee gate
  • Commitment — Legal docs, commitment letter
  • Closing — Fund transfer, escrow, post-close
  • Post-Investment Monitoring — Updates, board seats

Each transition requires: mandatory fields, approval gates, timestamp/audit, notifications.

Proof

At KKR, lived this pipeline daily. Built IC presentations = decision gates. Without defined gates, deals die in purgatory.

Integration Layer — Where CRM Projects Actually Fail
▼

Most CRM failures are integration failures, not feature gaps. Real complexity lives here:

  • Custodian feeds: Pershing, IB, private banks — proprietary APIs, FIX protocol, flat-file, 6-month onboarding
  • Market data: Bloomberg, Reuters (realtime quotes, corp actions)
  • Document management: Secure storage, version control, client portal access
  • Accounting: FundCount, Addepar, Masttro (cash flow, NAV, performance)
  • Client communication: RFO distribution, reporting automation

Why This Matters

Data quality in = data quality out. A broken custodian feed cascades through reporting, compliance, decision-making.

Proof

STT Mall — integrated Klaviyo, Shopify, Supabase, SendGrid, multiple payment/logistics APIs into unified pipeline. Each integration had unique quirks; coordinating them was the hard part.

AI Proposals for Discussion

Context Alert

3x more family offices leveraging AI in 2025 vs 2024. Top investment priorities 2026: AI/automation and data/reporting. Expect agentic interfaces, rise of 'hybrid operator' roles. This is the role Arthur is interviewing for.

1 Relationship Intelligence Layer ▼

Problem

As Raffles scales, institutional knowledge about relationships gets lost. Who introduced whom? Co-investment patterns? Warm intros vs. cold?

AI Opportunity

Automatically capture and map connections from:

  • Email flows (who introduced, tone, frequency)
  • Meeting notes (topics, outcomes, follow-ups)
  • Deal activity (co-investors, JV partners, syndications)

Result: Augment RM memory. When new RM joins, instant context on relationship strength, dynamics, deal appetite.

Privacy-First Implementation

On-premise/private cloud. No client data leaves org. Rule-based matching first, ML only for pattern recognition on anonymised metadata.

Industry Precedent

DealCloud, 4Degrees built around this. Salesforce Einstein in FSC does similar.

Arthur's Proof

Built behavioural scoring for 55K+ subscribers (STT Mall). Same pattern: capture implicit signals, surface insights at decision point.

2 Intelligent Client Onboarding & KYC Workflow ▼

Problem

Multi-jurisdiction onboarding complexity. Document collection, identity verification, beneficial ownership, source-of-wealth. Each family = 2-10 entities across 3-4 jurisdictions.

Manual: 2-4 weeks, back-and-forth, compliance overhead.

AI Solution

  • Identify needed docs by family structure + jurisdictions
  • Extract & validate data from submitted documents
  • Flag anomalies (beneficial ownership chains, PEP matches)
  • Audit trail for regulatory inspection

Privacy-First

On-premise OCR, no external uploads. Human-in-the-loop for compliance decisions (AI recommends, compliance approves).

Why Now

UAE/Rwanda expansion multiplies complexity. Scaling with manual processes = bottleneck.

3 AI-Powered Client Insights for RFO App ▼

Problem

RFO app on 3rd upgrade. Architecture is stable, but feature stagnation = vulnerability. Clients expect insights, not just dashboards.

What's Next?

AI-powered insight engine:

  • Personalised insights — Portfolio-relevant news, ESG flags, sector trends
  • Event/opportunity alerts by mandate — IPO coming in client's sector? SIFT match for new fund?
  • Natural language queries — "Show me my HK real estate exposure across all entities" (not dropdown hell)
  • Performance analytics — AI-generated summaries vs. raw numbers

Privacy-Engineered

AI runs within Raffles infrastructure. Client queries never leave secure environment.

Competitive Advantage

Most FO apps are dashboards. AI-powered insight engine becomes retention tool + basis for premium service tier.

4 Cross-Entity Reporting Automation ▼

Problem

Single UHNW family might have 10+ entities across 4 jurisdictions. Consolidated view requires pulling from multiple custodians, reconciling currencies, matching entities.

Reality: Every RM hour on manual reconciliation = hour not on relationships.

AI-Powered Solution

  • Automated ingestion from custodian feeds (Pershing, IB, etc.)
  • AI-powered reconciliation — Learn each custodian's format, match to entity hierarchy
  • Anomaly detection — Flag missing accounts, mismatched balances
  • Currency harmonisation — Spot-rate vs. historical, audit trail

Why This Matters

#1 operational pain point for MFOs. Every RM hour on manual reconciliation = hour not on relationships. AI handles the drudgery, RMs do client work.

Arthur's Proof

Integrated Klaviyo, Shopify, Supabase, SendGrid, payment/logistics APIs into unified pipeline (STT Mall). Each API had its quirks; unified ingestion layer handled translation.

February 9, 2026

Revo Digital Gets SFC Type 4 & Type 9 Licences

Digital asset portfolio services in Hong Kong — first digital MFO in Asia

View Details

Regulatory Milestone: Revo Digital (Raffles' digital asset subsidiary) received SFC (Securities and Futures Commission, Hong Kong) Type 4 (advising on securities) and Type 9 (asset management) licences.

Product Capabilities: Type 4 + 9 = digital asset portfolio advisory + discretionary asset management. Can manage portfolios of digital assets alongside traditional securities.

Competitive Position: One of the first institutions in Asia to combine digital + traditional portfolio management under a single MFO (Multi-Asset Family Office) license.

Custody Partnership: Revo uses Zodia Custody for secure digital asset safekeeping.

CRM Requirement: System must accommodate digital assets, their unique market data, custody arrangements, and regulatory reporting for Hong Kong MFO operations.
